See Toronto in a Single Day

A compact plan to experience skyline views, great food, and the waterfront.

Why this guide?

Toronto combines modern architecture and diverse neighbourhoods. In one day you can enjoy panoramic views from the CN Tower, lunch at 360 Restaurant, and walk the Harbourfront boardwalk.

Highlights

CN Tower

Start with an observation! By getting a ticket to CN Tower, you can access observation level and enjoy the view. Don't forget to visit the gift shop!

360 Restaurant

Rib or chicken, doesn't matter, every ingredient is organic! Enjoy your food while enjoying the view.

Harbourfront

Walk the boardwalk, visit the Power Plant gallery, or take a short ferry to the islands for golden-hour views.

Half-Day Plans

Morning to Afternoon

Morning: CN Tower & Roundhouse Park. Lunch: St. Lawrence Market. Afternoon: Distillery District shops and galleries.

Afternoon to Evening

Afternoon: Harbourfront Centre and the boardwalk. Dinner: Queen West or Chinatown. Evening: Sunset ferry to the islands for photos.
